Description

The AirPods 4 mark a significant evolution in Apple's standard-earbud line. Built around the powerful H2 headphone chip, they're designed to deliver richer, more immersive sound, smoother integration with Apple devices, and smarter capabilities than before.

Here are some of the features:

  • Immersive, theatre-like sound: With a custom high-excursion driver and high dynamic range amplifier, these earbuds are built to deliver deeper bass, clearer mids and highs, and an overall more refined audio profile.

  • Personalised Spatial Audio with dynamic head tracking: You get a three-dimensional soundscape, the effect is that audio feels like it's coming from around you rather than inside your head. Great for music, shows, gaming.

  • Smarter and clearer calls: With features like Voice Isolation (which reduces background noise) and advanced beamforming mics, the AirPods 4 are optimized for real-world environments.

  • Refined design and comfort: The shape has been re-worked for better fit and stability, with a shorter stem and a refined contour to make them comfortable for all-day use.

  • Two variants to choose from: The standard model, and a version with Active Noise Cancellation (ANC). The ANC version brings a premium level of noise control even though the design remains open-fit.

  • Smaller, smarter charging case: he case is more compact (over 10% smaller by volume compared to previous generations), supports USB-C charging, and for the ANC model also supports wireless charging and integrates a speaker for Find My features.

  • Built for everyday ruggedness: Rated IP54 for dust, sweat and water resistance, suitable for workouts, commuters, daily use.

  • Battery life:
    • Up to 30 hours of listening time with the case
    • Up to 5 hours of listening time on a single charge
    • Up to 4 hours of listening time with Active Noise Cancellation on a single charge

  • An instant connection: AirPods 4 pair immediately with every device connected to your Apple Account. Just place AirPods near your iPhone or iPad and tap Connect.
